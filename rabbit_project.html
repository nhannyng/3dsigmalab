3D Printed Rabbit Lamb From "homemade" PETG Filament

The decision to manufacture our own filament took shape when we realized that simply having the material wouldn't fulfill our vision; we needed to bring it to life through 3D printing. So, we embarked on an exciting journey to create a unique product—a lamp with a whimsical rabbit-shaped shade, inspired by a model we discovered online.

Our endeavor began with numerous iterations of the rabbit design. We were determined to find the perfect balance between aesthetics and functionality, ensuring it could be 3D printed with durability to withstand minor impacts. Step 1 involved rigorous testing to ensure our design was solid and ready for production.

Next came Step 2, where we designed an elegant wooden stand to complement the rabbit. We scoured the market to find a manufacturer who could bring our stand design to reality, ensuring it matched the quality and craftsmanship we envisioned.

In Step 3, we faced the challenge of scaling the rabbit design appropriately, making sure it seamlessly integrated with the wooden stand. Precision was key, as we wanted the entire lamp to come together harmoniously.

Now it's time to print (with the filaments that we produced earlier of course)!

Step 4 was all about gathering the necessary electrical components to illuminate our rabbit lamp. We sourced high-quality components to ensure the final product would not only look great but also provide functional lighting.

Finally, in Step 5, it was time to bring our vision to life. With all the pieces in hand, we meticulously assembled the lamp, carefully merging the 3D-printed rabbit with the wooden stand and integrating the lighting elements. The result was a stunning, functional work of art that showcased our dedication to craftsmanship and innovation.

Our journey from filament production to the creation of a unique rabbit-shaped lamp was a rewarding experience that not only allowed us to explore new horizons in 3D printing but also resulted in a captivating piece of functional art
